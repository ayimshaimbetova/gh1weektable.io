Learn English with serials
Many people prefer learning English through movies and TV series to other learning formats. Why?
There are some reasons:
  1. You are learning and having fun at the same time
  2. You develop your English listening skills
  3. You increase your vocabulary
  4. You learn phrasal verbs, idioms, colloquial forms of words and slang
  5. You learn the correct pronunciation
  6. You learn to understand the humor of native speakers
  7. You study the characteristics of another culture

"The Big Bang Theory"
The main characters of the series are young physicists Leonard Hofstadter and Sheldon Cooper - typical representatives of geek culture.
